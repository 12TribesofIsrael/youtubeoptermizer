# AI Bible Gospels — Brand Colors

## Primary Palette
| Color         | Hex       | Usage                                      |
|---------------|-----------|---------------------------------------------|
| Deep Navy     | #0A0A2A   | Backgrounds, base layers                    |
| Pure Black    | #0A0A0A   | Text cards, overlays                        |
| Divine Gold   | #E8C46B   | Headlines, accents, logo glow               |
| Warm Amber    | #D4A04A   | Secondary highlights, light beams           |
| Bronze        | #8B5E3C   | Skin tone reference, warm accents           |

## Supporting Colors
| Color         | Hex       | Usage                                      |
|---------------|-----------|---------------------------------------------|
| Soft Gold     | #F5DEB3   | Body text on dark backgrounds               |
| Dark Brown    | #3B2314   | Shadows, depth                              |
| Cloud White   | #F0E6D2   | Parting cloud effects, subtle highlights    |

## Lighting & Mood
- **Style**: Dramatic chiaroscuro
- **Key light**: Golden/amber breaking through darkness
- **Shadows**: Deep navy to black gradients

## Typography
- **Font style**: Bold serif with subtle gold glow
- **Title text**: Divine Gold (#E8C46B) on Pure Black (#0A0A0A)
- **Body text**: Soft Gold (#F5DEB3) on Deep Navy (#0A0A2A)
