2. Paste into the textarea at `https://tribesofisrael--ai-bible-gospels-web.modal.run/custom/`.
3. Click **Generate Scenes with Claude AI**.
4. Review the generated scenes. Check:
   - Scene 1's narration contains the hook line **verbatim** (especially the phrase "Read [Scripture] slowly"). If it was paraphrased, edit the narration to match the script exactly — the share-rate lift depends on that wording.

6. Download the MP4.

8. After 24h, grab the post URL and day-1 numbers, paste them back to Claude to log in the Updates section of `docs/viral-formula-deuteronomy-28.md`.
