As of 2026-04-29, aibiblegospels.com is positioned as a **faith-tech tools brand** — software/services for ministers, streamers, and missions — not just an AI Bible content publisher.

**Hero copy (live on site):** "Faith-tech for ministers, streamers, and missions."
**Subhead:** "We build tools that serve the gospel. Live trackers, stream automation, ministry websites, prayer walls. Software in service of the calling."
**CTAs:** "See the flagship" (Faith Walk Live) + "Work with us" (client services).

**Why:** This is a real strategic shift, not just copy polish. The parent brand AI Bible Gospels now has two arms:
1. **Content** — the YouTube channel, Shorts, IG/FB posts (existing)
2. **Tools / agency** — building faith-tech for other ministries (NEW positioning)

Faith Walk Live (faithwalklive.com) is the **flagship case study** — it proves the toolset works. The "Work with us" CTA opens the door to client work.

**How to apply:** When writing any AI Bible Gospels copy, social post, AEO description, or platform-review submission going forward:
- Lead with "faith-tech" framing, not just "AI Bible content"
- Name the four service areas: live trackers, stream automation, ministry websites, prayer walls
- Mention Faith Walk Live as the flagship/case study
- Use "Software in service of the calling" as the tagline phrase (keep it consistent across all surfaces for AEO entity-resolution)
- Audience to address: ministers / streamers / missions, not just Bible content viewers
