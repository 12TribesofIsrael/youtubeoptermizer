# AI Bible Gospels — YouTube Optimization Project Plan

## Goal
Transform AI BIBLE GOSPELS from 5.8K subs / 764K views into a discoverable, algorithm-friendly channel that consistently grows through search, suggested, and Shorts feed traffic.

## Unfair Advantage
The 12 Tribes identity / Hebrew Israelite prophecy angle is deeply differentiated. AI Bible Stories goes broad; we go deep. Don't dilute this.
