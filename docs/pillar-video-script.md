## SECTION 2: WHO ARE THE ISRAELITES? (0:30–2:00)

[VISUAL: Animated genealogy tree glowing gold — Noah at the top, branching down through Shem, Abraham, Isaac, Jacob. Each name appears with golden text.]

**NARRATION:**

To understand the 12 Tribes, you must understand where they came from.

Noah had three sons — Ham, Shem, and Japheth. Through Shem came Abraham. The Most High God said through Abraham He would establish His chosen seed, and through them all the nations of the world would be blessed.

Abraham had Isaac. Isaac had Jacob. And Jacob's name was changed to Israel, because he wrestled with an angel of God.

[VISUAL: Jacob kneeling, golden light surrounding him. Text: "JACOB = ISRAEL"]

Jacob had twelve sons — Reuben, Simeon, Levi, Judah, Dan, Naphtali, Gad, Asher, Issachar, Zebulun, Joseph, and Benjamin. These twelve sons became the 12 Tribes of Israel.

Deuteronomy chapter 7, verse 6 says: "For thou art a holy people unto the LORD thy God: the LORD thy God hath chosen thee to be a special people unto himself, above all people that are upon the face of the earth."

[VISUAL: Scripture text appears on screen — Deuteronomy 7:6]

These were God's chosen people. He gave them His commandments. He gave them His laws. He dealt with no other nation the way He dealt with Israel.

But Israel disobeyed.

[VISUAL: Dark transition — temple crumbling, flames, exile imagery]

Second Kings chapter 17, verses 17 and 18: "They caused their sons and their daughters to pass through the fire, and used divination and enchantments, and sold themselves to do evil in the sight of the LORD. Therefore the LORD was very angry with Israel, and removed them out of his sight: there was none left but the tribe of Judah only."

The Northern Kingdom — ten of the twelve tribes — was carried away into captivity by the Assyrians in 721 BC. Only the Southern Kingdom of Judah, Benjamin, and half of Levi remained. But not for long.

And they dwelt there until the latter time. They are still in their geographical regions today.

## SECTION 5: TRIBE BY TRIBE BREAKDOWN (5:00–15:00)

[VISUAL: Title card — "THE 12 TRIBES REVEALED" with golden tribal symbols]

### REUBEN — The Seminole Indians (5:00–5:50)

[VISUAL: Seminole warriors in traditional dress. Mountains of Quito. Map highlighting Florida.]

Genesis 49, verses 3 and 4: "Reuben, thou art my firstborn, my might, and the beginning of my strength, the excellency of dignity, and the excellency of power. Unstable as water, thou shalt not excel."

The Seminole Indians possessed great dignity in their dress and their honor. They were the only civilized Indian nation who refused to participate in the enslavement of the Negroes. They fought alongside their brothers in slave revolts. They were unstable as water because they were nomadic — moving from the mountains of Quito in South America to the coast, to the islands, and finally to Florida.

The Seminoles were the greatest enemy the Europeans ever encountered. They never willingly signed a peace treaty.

---

### SIMEON — The Dominicans (5:50–6:30)

[VISUAL: Island of Hispaniola. A dividing line splitting the island in two. Dark, mystical imagery.]

Genesis 49, verses 5 through 7: "Simeon and Levi are brethren; instruments of cruelty are in their habitations. I will divide them in Jacob, and scatter them in Israel."

Jacob called Simeon and Levi brothers — not just because they were his sons, but because in the last days they would dwell together in one place: the island of Hispaniola. But the Most High said He would divide them — and today that island is split between the Dominican Republic and Haiti.

The Dominicans practice Brujeria. The instruments of cruelty are the witchcraft that has taken root in their habitations.

---

### LEVI — The Haitians (6:30–7:10)

[VISUAL: Haitian imagery. Voodoo rituals. Ancient priests contrasted with modern spiritual practices.]

Malachi chapter 2, verses 8 and 9: "Ye are departed out of the way; ye have caused many to stumble at the law; ye have corrupted the covenant of Levi."

The tribe of Levi were the priests of Israel. They were holy unto the Most High. But because of slavery, they adopted Voodoo — mixing the worship of the Most High with pagan idolatry. And because they stopped teaching God's commandments, a curse was placed upon them. To this day, Haiti is considered the most impoverished nation in the Western Hemisphere.

---

### JUDAH — African Americans (7:10–8:20)

[VISUAL: Cinematic scenes of African American history — slavery, civil rights marches, churches, modern day. Lion imagery. Golden crown.]

Genesis 49, verses 8 through 10: "Judah, thou art he whom thy brethren shall praise: thy hand shall be in the neck of thine enemies. Judah is a lion's whelp. The sceptre shall not depart from Judah, until Shiloh come."

This is the tribe of the King. Christ Himself came from the tribe of Judah — Hebrews chapter 7, verse 14 confirms it.

"Thy hand shall be in the neck of thine enemies" — this gives you the exact geographical location: North America. The daughter of Babylon. The American Negro has fought for everything — education, civil rights, desegregation, housing, truth. They built this nation and received nothing in return.

[VISUAL: Jeremiah 22:13 text — "Woe unto him that useth his neighbour's service without wages"]

Judah is the lion's whelp — fearless, but distracted. In the sixties they rose up like an old lion, forming movements for freedom. But drugs were pushed into the community. The plot to keep Judah asleep was deliberate.

But Genesis 49:10 says the sceptre shall not depart from Judah. The King of Kings comes from this tribe. And Zechariah 12:7 says: "The LORD shall save the tents of Judah first."

### ZEBULUN — Guatemalans to Panamanians / The Mayans (8:20–9:00)

[VISUAL: Panama Canal. Mayan pyramids. Central American landscapes.]

Genesis 49, verse 13: "Zebulun shall dwell at the haven of the sea; and he shall be for a haven of ships."

The haven of ships — the Panama Canal. Opened in 1914, connecting the Atlantic and Pacific oceans. The most important shipping lane in the world. And it sits right in the land of Zebulun.

Deuteronomy 33:18 says: "Rejoice, Zebulun, in thy going out; and Issachar, in thy tents." Zebulun and Issachar lived near each other — and the Mayans would travel out of their cities to meet the Aztecs at places of worship: Teotihuacan, Chichen Itza, Palenque.

---

### ISSACHAR — The Mexicans / The Aztecs (9:00–9:45)

[VISUAL: Mexican laborers. Aztec pyramids. Burro with two satchels.]

Genesis 49, verses 14 and 15: "Issachar is a strong ass couching down between two burdens. He bowed his shoulder to bear, and became a servant unto tribute."

The two burdens — the long hours and the low wages. The Mexicans are known throughout the world as some of the hardest working people, laboring ten to fifteen hours a day for almost nothing. One of the most familiar symbols of Mexico is a burro with two large satchels on either side — two burdens, exactly as Jacob prophesied.

"And he saw that rest was good, and the land that it was pleasant." Mexico is one of the world's favorite vacation spots. The land is beautiful and fertile.

The Aztecs also had superior knowledge of times and seasons — and First Chronicles 12:32 says the children of Issachar were "men that had understanding of the times."

---

### GAD — The North American Indians (9:45–10:30)

[VISUAL: Native American warriors. Battle of Wounded Knee. Vast American landscapes. Feathered headdress.]

Genesis 49, verse 19: "Gad, a troop shall overcome him: but he shall overcome at the last."

The troop that overcame Gad was the United States Cavalry. From Plymouth Rock in 1620 to the Battle of Wounded Knee in 1890, over 77 million Indians were killed to establish church and state in America.

But Jacob said — he shall overcome at the last.

Deuteronomy 33:20 says: "Blessed be he that enlargeth Gad: he dwelleth as a lion." Despite the genocide, the Most High enlarged Gad with many sons and daughters. There are over 300 Native nations that make up the tribe of Gad. And Gad took the best part of the land — the mainland of North America.

---

### ASHER — Colombians, Brazilians, Venezuelans / The Inca (10:30–11:10)

[VISUAL: South American landscapes — Amazon, Andes, oil fields, coffee plantations, carnival. Inca ruins.]

Genesis 49, verse 20: "Out of Asher his bread shall be fat, and he shall yield royal dainties."

The lands of Asher — Brazil, Colombia, Peru, Venezuela — are the world's biggest producers of cattle, coffee, cocoa, sugar, rice, corn, and fruits. The bread is fat. The land overflows with abundance.

Deuteronomy 33:25 says: "Thy shoes shall be iron and brass." The Hebrew word for iron is Ba-ra-zal — Brazil. Asher stands on some of the richest mineral resources on earth — iron, brass, chromium, nickel, tin, and copper.

Deuteronomy 33:24: "Let Asher be blessed with children." Brazil has the largest population of children in the world — but Lamentations 5:3 says: "We are orphans and fatherless, our mothers are as widows." Millions of street children in Brazil, destitute, without hope.

---

### NAPHTALI — Polynesians, Hawaiians, Samoans, Fijians (11:10–11:50)

[VISUAL: Pacific islands. Polynesian dancers. Ocean waves. Lush tropical landscapes.]

Genesis 49, verse 21: "Naphtali is a hind let loose: he giveth goodly words."

A hind is a deer — free, wild, bounding. The Polynesians are some of the most free and vibrant people in the world. Their dancing, their ceremonies, their customs — free as a hind let loose.

"He giveth goodly words" — Aloha. Kia Kaha. Fa'amalosi. The people of the Pacific Islands are known for their good words and their respect.

Deuteronomy 33:23: "Possess thou the west and the south." The Hebrew word for west is Yam — meaning a large body of water westward. Naphtali would possess land in the southern hemisphere, surrounded by a large body of water to the west. History shows they were in Chile first, and are now scattered across the South Pacific.

### EPHRAIM — The Puerto Ricans (11:50–12:40)

[VISUAL: Puerto Rico — beaches, El Morro fortress, Boriqua culture, sugarcane fields. Fruitful tropical imagery.]

Genesis 49, verse 22: "Joseph is a fruitful bough, even a fruitful bough by a well; whose branches run over the wall."

The name Ephraim means "I am fruitful." The native Indians of Puerto Rico called themselves Boriqua — a Hebrew word meaning "Brave." They called their island Borinquen — "Land of the Brave Lords." The language spoken on that island before the Spanish invasion was Hebrew.

Genesis 49:23: "The archers have sorely grieved him, and shot at him, and hated him." The archers were the Spanish conquistadors. When Ponce de Leon arrived in 1508, he had six thousand Boriqua Indians massacred because they protested slave working conditions. Out of forty thousand, only four thousand remained.

But Ephraim's fruitfulness kept the tribe alive — because the Most High's blessing was upon them.

### MANASSEH — The Cubans (12:40–13:10)

[VISUAL: Cuba — Havana, sugarcane, Caribbean waters. Separated island.]

The name Manasseh means "made to forget." Genesis 48:19 says Manasseh "shall become a people, and he also shall be great: but truly his younger brother shall be greater than he."

Manasseh — the Cubans — were separated from their brothers on a nearby island, exactly as the name prophesied. The Spanish explorer Montezinos was told by the Seminoles that "the tribe of Joseph lived on an island nearby." Ephraim in Puerto Rico. Manasseh in Cuba. Two brothers, two islands, one prophecy.

### BENJAMIN — The Jamaicans and West Indians (13:10–14:00)

[VISUAL: Jamaica — Blue Mountains, reggae culture, Maroon warriors in the hills. Wolf imagery.]

Genesis 49, verse 27: "Benjamin shall ravin as a wolf: in the morning he shall devour the prey, and at night he shall divide the spoil."

The tribe of Benjamin, along with Judah and Levi, were sold into captivity by Africans and Arabs, transported on slave ships to Jamaica and the West Indian islands.

During slavery, the Maroons — the word Maroon comes from the Latin word for wolf — were runaway slaves who fled to the mountains of Jamaica. The Europeans could not subdue them because they knew the land and believed in liberty or death.

"Benjamin shall ravin as a wolf" — the word ravin means to speak boldly. And the tribe of Benjamin howls through their music. Reggae. They sing about Babylon falling. They sing about the Israelites waking up. They sing about Christ being a Black man. Most reggae artists get their inspiration directly from Psalms and Revelation.

At night, he shall divide the spoil — when the rulership changes, Benjamin and the other tribes will divide the riches and wealth of the nations. Jeremiah 30:16.

## SECTION 6: THE PROOF — THE MONTEZINOS ACCOUNT (14:15–15:15)

[VISUAL: Dramatic reenactment — Spanish explorer meeting Indigenous people in mountains. They raise their hands and speak. Hebrew text appears on screen.]

**NARRATION:**

Now let me give you one piece of evidence that should remove all doubt.

In the 1600s, a Spanish explorer named Antonio Montezinos traveled to the mountains of Quito in South America. There he met a group of Indigenous people — the Seminole Indians.

And to his utter astonishment, they stood before him and said: "Shema, Yisroel, Adonai Elohenu Adonai Ehad."

[VISUAL: Hebrew text glowing gold — "שְׁמַע יִשְׂרָאֵל"]

"Hear, O Israel, the Lord our God, the Lord is One." They had recited, in Hebrew, the fundamental creed of Judaism.

They told Montezinos they were of the tribe of Reuben. And that the tribe of Joseph lived on an island nearby.

This account is documented in the book "Lost Tribes and Promised Lands" by Ronald Sanders, pages 363 to 365.

The evidence has always been there. It was hidden — but the Most High said in the last days, knowledge would increase.
