## Competitor 1: BibleProject

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Subscribers | 5.3M |
| Niche | Broad Bible education, animated explainers |
| Content Format | Long-form animated explainers (8-20 min) |
| Upload Frequency | 1-2x per week |

### What They Do Better
- **Evergreen long-form content** — their top videos are 8-18 minute animated overviews posted years ago that still get millions of views
- **Professional animation** — consistent, high-quality 2D animation style
- **Playlist funnels** — organized by book of the Bible, making binge-watching easy
- **Titles are timeless** — "The Book of Genesis Overview" ranks forever in search

### Top Video Patterns
- "Overview: [Book of Bible]" format — simple, searchable, evergreen
- "What is [concept]?" question hooks
- Consistent thumbnail style: white background, bold illustration, book name

### Thumbnail Style
- Clean white/cream backgrounds
- Bold illustrated characters (not photorealistic)
- Book name or concept as large text
- Very different from our dark/cinematic style (no conflict)

### Tactics to Steal
1. **Create 4-6 "Overview" style videos** for our niche: "The Complete Story of the 12 Tribes," "Who Are the Lost Tribes of Israel? Full Breakdown"
2. **Playlist funnels by tribe** — one playlist per tribe, makes binge-watching easy
3. **Evergreen titles** that rank in search for years, not just trending topics

## Title Formula Cheat Sheet

Stolen from the best performers across all competitors:

```
Question Hooks (Bible in Black style):
  "Why Did [Biblical Figure] [Unexpected Action]?"
  "What Really Happened at [Event]?"
  "Who Are the [Group] and Why Does It Matter?"

Mystery/Revelation (AI Bible Sagas style):
  "The Hidden Truth About [Tribe/Event]"
  "[Topic]: What the Bible REALLY Says"
  "The Shocking Story of [Event] — Most Christians Don't Know This"

Specificity + Search (BibleProject style):
  "The Complete Story of [Tribe/Book/Event]"
  "[Book] Explained: What You Need to Know"
  "The [Tribe] — Full Breakdown"

Emotional Triggers (AI Bible Stories style):
  "When God [Dramatic Action]"
  "The Day [Something Shocking Happened]"
  "They Never Told You About [Hidden Truth]"
```
